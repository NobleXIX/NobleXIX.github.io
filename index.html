Welcome to How2Skate

This is a passion-project (also school project) website dedicated to skateboarding. This website aims at familiarizing people of all backgrounds with skateboarding as a hobby, and also the history of it. A more particular aim of this site is to teach people how to skate and point them in the direction of other resources should they desire to continue learning.
