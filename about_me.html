About the developer... me!

Hello! I'm Jacob, the developer of this here website. Here's a somewhat silly picture of me before skating from a while back. I got into skateboarding in October of 2023 at the "behest of a friend of mine" and have enjoyed it ever since. In reality, I did it to impress a girl (still not sure if it worked). And though my reasons may be silly or seem like a poor foundation, it ironically catapulted me into what I discovered to be one of my favorite pastimes.

Regrettably, I haven't found much time to do so lately, but that's a-ok. Further, I am not the craziest or gnarliest skater, but those traits aren't imperative for the mission of this site. I just want to show that if I can skate, anybody can. Some other fun facts about me: my favorite band is Killswitch Engage, I am a Roman Catholic, I like to run, and I think mint chocolate chip ice cream is only good when it's green. Thank you for reading, and skate on!
