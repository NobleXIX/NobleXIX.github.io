Welcome to How2Skate

This is a passion-project (also school project) website dedicated to skateboarding. This website aims at familiarizing people of all backgrounds with skateboarding as a hobby, and also the history of it. A more particular aim of this site is to teach people how to skate and point them in the direction of other resources should they desire to continue learning. Here, you will learn everything a beginner (aspiring) skater should know about skateboarding.

Use the navigation menu above to access the different pages this website has to offer. Skating History will teach you about the history of skating, How to Skate is where you will learn how to skate (duh), Resources will direct you to some other resources for continued learning and potential skating equipment should you desire to continue learning, and finally, About the Dev will tell you a little bit about me and why I decided to create this website.

Enjoy!
